About MaterialBank

Our Mission

Making non-essential essentials accessible.

Inspired by the needs of our local community, our mission is to support academic success for marginalized youth through supplying school supplies, stationery, and other materials we consider non-essential essentials.

Organizations We've Donated To

A look at the communities and organizations MaterialBank has supported through school and art supply donations.

Pathways to Education Program

On July 7th, 2024, we donated hundreds of school supplies to the Pathways to Education Program at their Regent Park Community Health Centre site. This donation aims to support 560 high school students in downtown east core areas who are part of their Youth Enrichment Academy.

YWCA Toronto Girls Centre

On January 24th, 2024, we donated a wide range of school supplies such as binders and textbooks. YWCA Toronto Girls Centre provides mental health, educational, and leadership programs to help students thrive academically.

Kids of Incarcerated Parents (KIP Canada)

On August 9th, 2023, we donated 2,105 school and art supplies ranging from erasers to binders. KIP Canada supports children whose families are affected by the justice system.

Toronto Foundation for Student Success

On May 31, we donated 7 bags with over 2,000 items, totaling 2,852 materials. These donations supported the Beyond 3:30 after-school program for children ages 8–14.

The Centre for Addiction and Mental Health (CAMH)

On November 29th, 2023, we donated art supplies ranging from marker kits to oil pastels for CAMH’s Gifts of Light program, which helps support patient wellness and transition back into the community.
