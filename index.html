How We Work

Three Core Areas of Impact

Donations, awareness, and environmental responsibility all work together to strengthen educational equity.

Donations

Donations are fundamental to our mission. 100% of monetary donations go toward ensuring school supplies get to underserved communities, while item donations are collected through pop-ups, drop-off locations, mail, and pick-up for larger donors.

Awareness

Meaningful impact starts with understanding the communities we support. We prioritize listening directly to people and learning what different communities identify as their needs.

Eco-Friendly

Our model supports communities economically and environmentally by promoting second-hand use and helping reduce overconsumption through reuse.
