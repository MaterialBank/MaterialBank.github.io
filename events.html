Events

Our Art Workshops

  • On August 25, 2024, we hosted our second art workshop at The 519 Back to School Bash. We provided face painting, painting, colouring, Play-Doh and more. The 519 supports 2SLGBTQ+ youth.
  • On September 8, 2023, we hosted an art workshop at Daniels Spectrum. Activities included colouring, clay sculpting, watercolour, and origami. We also collected donations and participated in RP Vibeville’s community BBQ and backpack giveaway.

Other Events

  • On May 11, 2024, we set up a stand at a Granite Club student event and met many families.
  • Early March 2024, we attended the Canadian Youth Club Summit and won the Trailblazer Award ($1500).
  • On November 20, 2023, we attended an event at BMO First Canadian Place.
